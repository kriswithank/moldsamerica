Services

Part Fillings

Part filling simulations provide information about how the plastic will flow in part design, flow lines, gas traps with your suggested gating location. Additional simulations can be run to optimize gate location and size.

Mold Fillings

Mold filling simulations help determine if the part will fill with the proposed gate location and aid in checking that the cavity fill is balanced -- very important for family tools.

Complementary Presentation

Each simulation will have a full presentation that you can use to discuss with your clients. This interaction will help to keep your clients involved in the design or tooling process. Contact us if you would like to view a sample presentation.
